Rest bonuses

1. You can claim at least six hours as rest bonus in a single rest period session. A rest period is started by claiming RB0 in the usual way, with a photo of a receipt next to your odometer. The end of the period is claimed using code RB6.


2. You may not claim any other bonuses during your rest period.


3. You can rest where you want. So maybe in a hotel or outside anywhere. Just get receipts to prove you stayed in one place, even if you have to ride a short distance in between the times you claim for i.e. maybe a receipt from a 24 hour fuel station.


4. Rest periods can only be taken between 1200 noon on Saturday and 1200 noon on Sunday.
