How to claim a bonus

Example bonus

21Overbecks 65
 21 = Bonus code

 Overbecks = Bonus name

  65 = Bonus points
Coordinates:    N43° 51.9542' E125° 34.9472'
  • Question Bonus to answer for +50 Points!
  • Put the whole of your face in the picture. Helmet off or flipped up.
  • Put the whole of your bike in the picture

What to do

  • Take the same picture as shown in this book.
  • Your rally flag must be in every photo.
  • Your pillion (if you have one) must be in every photo.
  • Email your claims to ibaukebc@gmail.com

The Subject: line of the email should contain either four or five fields (Rider number, Bonus code, Odo reading, Claim time and optionally answer to question) separated by spaces only.

Examples:-   1 01 23072 1334
1 13 3072 1412 ploughman's lunch

The rally is scored automatically using the content of the Subject: line and the picture submitted. Check the content carefully before sending email. You must only email from your registered email address, the one used to register for the rally. Please tell Steve if you need to use a different address.
