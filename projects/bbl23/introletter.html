Scoring

On your return ensure all your emails have been cleared from your phone before the odo check when your rally finishes and no more claims will be accepted. You will be given a print out of your claims showing the reason any claims were unsuccessful.

READ VERY CAREFULLY TO UNDERSTAND POINTS SYSTEM

There are two types of bonuses

Scoring. Points are the value of that bonus. eg 122
Multipliers. Points are the multiplier times the points value of the last bonus claimed. eg x4

There are no points for a multiplier after a multiplier.

Be careful: If you claim a scoring bonus incorrectly you will also lose the following multiplier even if it is correctly claimed
