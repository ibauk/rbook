Introduction

At first glance this book doesn’t have many bonuses. You can use it for a nice day out visiting the ancient locations or you can unlock the puzzle and plan a winning route that you can also ride.

It could take you on the fantastic roads of the Brecons, Forest of Dean, Exmoor, Blackdown Hills, Cotswolds, Wilshire Downs, and New Forest. You will probably visit a World Heritage site


Remember it’s only a ride. Stop if you are tired.

Ride safe, have a good day out and come back in one piece.
